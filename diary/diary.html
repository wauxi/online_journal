January 4th 2017

Chloe woke me up earlyy- like 4am early! I mean all in good reason and it's not like a could be mad at her long- all she had to do was look at me with those bright blue eyes and I'd be a flustered Max puddle ready to wait on her hand and foot... not even worth trying to stay in bed.

It was worth it though.

We stumbled out into the freezing cold air and at that point I almost gave up, tugging at her sleeve and complaining as the frozen air and snowflakes attacked my face.

But I'm glad the punk managed to convince me to go further and also put up with my whining like a kid on a car trip the whole way...

We walked further and yeah by this point I was really starting to enjoy myself. Apart from the cold the sky was so clear and littered with stars and the snow capped trees looked like something out of a video game!

Anyways after about fifteen minutes Chloe stopped me at the top of a hill. I was fully questioning it but as the sun came up I really started to understand why she had dragged my disoriented ass over here.

We both took a seat on the ridge of the cliff, Chloe had brought a blanket so we didn't get wet but's haha. She cuddled up to me and we sat and watched the sunrise together, it was so cheesy or as Chloe would say "mushy" but I'm such a sucker for that and I loved every minute.

Now I guess it's time for breakfast before I try snowboarding! Oooooor butt sledding with your clumsy legs strapped onto a slippery board you cant control... yeah hehhh this is a bad idea!
